About Us

Taste Of My Skin is an annual event that was initially started to inspire confidence within people with Albinism, to pursue their dreams, whatever they may be, but with emphasis on performance arts. Our Founder Jairus Ongetta is an award winning poet, singer/song writer, Actor, Film maker, Radio and TV host, Fashion icon and Model among other things. It was this rich background that cultivated a passion to see other People With Albinism break barriers of Stigma and Discrimination.
